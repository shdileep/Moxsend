# Moxsend 🚀

> **AI-Powered Outreach for the Modern Team**

Generate high-converting cold emails instantly with AI-driven personalization, multi-touch sequences, and psychological insights.

---

## Overview

Moxsend is a B2B email automation platform that enables sales, marketing, and recruiting teams to create personalized outreach workflows at scale. By leveraging Google's Gemini AI, Moxsend generates contextually relevant cold emails, subject lines, opening hooks, and follow-up sequences in seconds—without the tedious manual copywriting.

**Key Capability**: Transform product descriptions and target audience parameters into 3 high-converting email variants, complete with Day 1/3/7 follow-up sequences and LinkedIn connection notes.

---

## Features

### 🎯 Core Email Generation
- **Multi-variant generation**: Creates 3 unique email variations per campaign
- **AI-powered subject lines**: Multiple subject line options with psychological triggers
- **Opening hooks**: Engaging opening lines designed to increase email open rates
- **Psychological insights**: AI-generated insights explaining the psychological strategy behind each email
- **Email sequences**: Automated Day 1, Day 3, and Day 7 follow-up templates

### 📧 Multi-Channel Outreach
- **Email variants**: Main outreach email with customizable tone and goal
- **LinkedIn integration**: 300-character LinkedIn connection notes alongside email campaigns
- **B2B focus**: Templated approaches for SaaS, enterprise, recruitment, partnerships, and more

### 💾 Lead Management
- **CSV/Excel import**: Upload leads in bulk and process them automatically
- **Lead status tracking**: Monitor generation progress (idle, generating, completed, failed)
- **Batch processing**: Generate emails for multiple leads sequentially
- **CSV export**: Download generated emails and sequences for use in your CRM

### ⚙️ Customization Controls
- **Tone selection**: Professional, Friendly, Persuasive, Startup Style, Enterprise, Casual
- **Campaign goals**: Sales Outreach, Demo Booking, Product Launch, Follow-Up, Investor Pitch, Partnership
- **Quick templates**: Pre-configured templates for common B2B scenarios (SaaS, Ecommerce, Security, Recruitment, etc.)
- **Email improvement**: Iteratively refine generated emails with specific feedback

### 🎨 Modern UI/UX
- **Glass-morphism design**: Clean, modern interface with glassmorphic cards
- **Real-time feedback**: Toast notifications and loading states
- **Structured viewing**: Modal-based lead detail view with all outreach variants
- **Copy-to-clipboard**: One-click email copying for quick implementation
